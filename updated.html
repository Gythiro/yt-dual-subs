v3.4.0 2026-07-10 You are here当前版本
  • NEW

    Smart sentence mode. When a track can't be translated by YouTube, fragmented auto-captions are first rebuilt into full sentences, then translated — much more natural results.

    智能整句翻译。当整轨翻译不可用时，先把碎片化的自动字幕重组成完整句子再翻译，译文自然得多。

  • NEW

    Auto engine. Picks the best available engine per video and falls back gracefully; you can still pin one manually.

    自动引擎。按视频自动选择最佳翻译方式并平滑降级，也可以手动固定某一种。

  • IMPROVED改进

    Sturdier under rate limits: request queue with backoff, retry for half-translated lines, "…" placeholder instead of mismatched lines.

    限流下更稳：请求队列+退避重试、半翻句自动补翻、用"…"占位替代错位配对。
